Welcome to COVIDO

COVIDO is a charitable organisation created with the intention of helping individuals and families who have been hit the worst by the COVID-19 pandemic. As you might already know, our country suffered tremendously during the second wave of this pandemic and every affected family was not in the economic position to fight this virus. Hence, we intend to direct the donations we receive to the people who are in need of funds at the moment for hospital charges, medicines, etc. to recover from COVID-19, along with those families who have lost their earning members, in order to ensure that they have the financial support to get back on their feet. Every penny of the donations we receive will be straightaway directed to the people who are in dire need of it. We urge you to donate whatever amount you can afford to. Every single rupee matters.
